# About me

I'm Tony Law, a senior full-stack engineer focused on fintech, cross-border
payments, payment gateways, and AI-assisted financial tooling. I have built
production systems across web, mobile, backend APIs, financial product workflows,
monitoring, and internal platforms.

My recent work sits at the intersection of software engineering and markets:
options research tools, AI-assisted U.S. equity analysis, publishing automation,
and product infrastructure for financial workflows. I care about systems that are
useful in the real world: clear ownership, observable behavior, reliable delivery,
and interfaces that make complex financial decisions easier to reason about.

Before building independently, I worked on cross-border financial products,
payment infrastructure, and R&D management systems. That experience shaped how I
think about product engineering: software should survive compliance, operations,
regional rollout, and actual user pressure, not just look clean in a demo.
